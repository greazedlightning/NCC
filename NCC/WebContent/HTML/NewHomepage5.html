Welcome to Newbury Cricket Club

There’s been a Newbury Cricket Club since 1822, and the modern sides first played at their home ground on Northcroft Playing Fields in 1947. Today we have three Thames Valley Cricket League sides, plus an occasional Sunday eleven, and a growing junior section catering for all ages from Under 11 upwards.

It’s a special place to play cricket, right in the town centre, with the river along one boundary, and Northcroft lane on the other, and the church spire in the background. In the evening the light across the outfield from a low sun setting beyond Northcroft Leisure Centre can be absolutely beautiful.

We are proud of our traditions at Newbury, and we like to blend competitive cricket with a friendly atmosphere on and off the pitch at all levels. We play to win and talent is rewarded with challenging league cricket, but not at the expense of the game’s traditional values of fair play and team spirit. And there is room for cricketers of all skill levels to practice and play in a team which suits their individual ambitions.

Our juniors are coached by level two ECB qualified coaches, with winter indoor nets for juniors and adults at the excellent Newbury College facilities, and summer nets and training at our own Northcroft cricket ground.

Join us for the 2015 season

Membership of our club is open to everyone, so if you would like to play in one of our teams or watch cricket in West Berkshire, why not consider joining, or just come down to the ground on match days and support your local team.

Senior Membership

All you have to do to join is read the Code of Conduct and submit the online Membership Form.Your application should quickly be approved by the committee. Ed Uden, Treasurer of the Club will then contact you with details for payment, either by cheque or by BACS if you prefer.

Alternatively you can come to a winter nets or training session at Newbury College as a trial. Bring your Membership application with you, and see how you fit in before deciding whether you would like to join.

The summer nets at our Northcroft ground are open to senior members at all times, but there is usually an organised club net every week on Tuesdays from around 6.00pm, when (weather permitting) you can be sure of finding players at the ground. Junior training is on Mondays, from 6.30 pm.

Junior Membership

To play in one of three junior teams or for coaching please email us. for details of joining and one of our club officers will be in touch with details. There may sometimes be a limit on the number of junior members dependant on facilities and numbers coaching staff.

Online forms and info

Membership form for senior sides Email us for Junior membership

Overseas Players: Whilst membership is open to all, if you are an overseas player, having been continuously resident in the UK for two of the last four years, you are very welcome to join the club, but it may not be always be possible for us to select you to play in League Matches. We are restricted by the League to playing one overseas player in each team. Please contact us to see if there are likely to be any overseas places available.

For more detailed information see our information pack.

The annual subscription for 2014 is £90, and the match fee if selected to play is an additional £10 per match. Students, and un-waged members get a 50% discount. Junior membership (£35) is open to anyone aged under 17 on 1st September in the preceding year.
