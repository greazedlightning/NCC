Newbury Cricket Club ties up with Serious Cricket

Following our recently announced partnership agreement with Serious Cricket, our brand new online cricket clothing store offers a wide range of playing and training teamwear, from playing shirts/trousers to hoodies, training tops and tracksuits. There is something for everyone. Serious Cricket will be our exclusive club supplier for the next 3 seasons and we are very pleased to be working closely with them on this.
