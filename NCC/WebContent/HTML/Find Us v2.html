Grounds

The home ground for Newbury 1st XI and Newbury 2nd XI is at Northcroft Lane, Newbury, RG14 1RS.

Directions

From M4 exit J13, A34 south, bearing left onto A339 signposted Newbury. At A4 roundabout take Town Centre (4th exit). Turn left at the Clock Tower into Northbrook St then first right at McDonalds into West St. After 100 yards turn left at Lion PH then after 200 yards turn right at T junction into Northcroft Lane and the ground is on the left.


Newbury 3 XI: The home ground for is at Park House School, Andover Road, Newbury RG14 6NQ

Directions

From M4 Junction 13 follow A339 through Newbury passing Sainsbury’s on the left. At the next roundabout take the A343 St John’s Rd signposted Andover. Continue straight on at next roundabout into Andover Rd. After 1.5 miles go straight on over a double mini roundabout next to the Gun and the Old Bell pubs. After 200 yards the ground is on your left, nearly opposite a Sainsbury's Local supermarket.

Summer nets

During the season, we use our dual net facilities which were installed only a few years ago. With the bowling machine also available to use (weather permitting), we welcome existing, new and prospective new members to our sessions. Details of the junior and senior net sessions are detailed below:

Junior Nets - Monday evenings from 6.30pm

Our Junior section managers and ECB qualified coaches run various levels of training sessions on Monday evenings at our Northcroft playing fields. For joining details please send us an email and one of the Club's officials will reply.

Senior Nets - Tuesday evenings

Our outdoor net facilities are used by senior team members on Tuesday evenings. The nets are in use typically from end of April until the end of the season.

The senior team members hone their cricketing skills through various levels of practice sessions and can also obtain one-to-one coaching from our qualified coaches.

Winter Nets - 2015

Senior Nets (including the older Under 15s and Under 17s) will be on Thursday evenings, between 8pm and 10pm at Newbury College, starting on 12th February and through to 23rd April, with no breaks in between.

Junior Nets will be on Thursday evenings between 6pm and 8pm at Newbury College, starting on 5th February and through to 30th April, apart from half-term (February 19th) and the Easter weeks of 9th and 16th April. Exact times for age groups will be confirmed nearer the time, but it is likely that the Under 11s will be at 6pm and Under 13s and younger Under 15s at 7pm.

The older Under 15s will be netting with the seniors, where there will be an Under 15 net as well as the Under 15s joining in with the senior nets, the bowling machine will also be available as per previous seasons.
