Grounds

The home ground for Newbury 1st XI and 2nd XI is at the address above. Newbury 3rd XI play home matches at Park House School ground.

Map and directions to Newbury Cricket Club

Directions to Newbury 1st XI and 2nd XI games

From M4 exit J13, A34 south, bearing left onto A339 signposted Newbury. At A4 roundabout take Town Centre (4th exit). Turn left at the Clock Tower into Northbrook St then first right at McDonalds into West St. After 100 yards turn left at Lion PH then after 200 yards turn right at T junction into Northcroft Lane and the ground is on the left.

Park House School (Newbury 3rd XI games)

Park House School, Andover Road, Newbury RG14 6NQ

From M4 Junction 13 follow A339 through Newbury passing Sainsbury’s on the left. At the next roundabout take the A343 St John’s Rd signposted Andover. Continue straight on at next roundabout into Andover Rd. After 1.5 miles go straight on over a double mini roundabout next to the Gun and the Old Bell pubs. After 200 yards the ground is on your left, nearly opposite a Total petrol station.
