Junior cricket at Newbury Cricket Club

Newbury Cricket Club runs teams at U11, U13, U15 and U19 level. Over the last few years the club has been very successful in coaching the young players and we have also been very successful in the integration our junior players into the senior teams.

All coaches and managers are DBS checked in accordance with our club policies.

The Under 11s play in the Berkshire Youth Cricket League, U11 Division 2 West.

The Under 13s play in the Berkshire Youth Cricket League, U13 Division 2 West.

The Under 15s play in the Berkshire Youth Cricket League, U15 Division 1 West.

The Under 19s play in the Berkshire Cricket T20 Blast.

To join or to make enquiries please send us an email and one of our club officers will get back with all the information.
