Newbury CC is recruiting for junior teams

Is your son or daughter the next Freddie Flintoff or Charlotte Edwards?

If so, why don't you get them to develop their cricket skills further at Newbury Cricket Club?

Junior cricket at Newbury Cricket Club

Newbury Cricket Club runs teams at U11, U13 and U15 level. Over the last few years the club has been very successful in coaching the young players, and we have also been very successful in the integration our junior players into the senior teams.

We have a team of experienced managers and club coaches, some of whom have been through our system as juniors themselves. All coaches and managers are CRB checked in accordance with our club policies.

The Under 11s play in the Berkshire Youth Cricket League, U11 Division 2 West.

Team Manager for our U11 side is John O'Sullivan. John is also the Chairman of the club and has been promoting youth cricket at the club for several years.

The Under 13s play in the Berkshire Youth Cricket League, U13 Division 2 West.

Team Manager for our U13 side is Stephen Gregory. Steve is also Junior Section Coordinator and Coach of the club looking after most aspects of the club's Junior zone.

The Under 15s play in the Berkshire Youth Cricket League, U15 Division 1 West.

Team Manager (temporary) for our U15 side is Charlie Anstey. Charlie has been the Secretary and Captain of the 1st XI side for 19 years at the club.

For all junior sides, winter nets is every Thursday from 5th February between 6pm and 8pm at Newbury College. The U11s start at 6pm and U13s start at 7pm.
The U15s start at 8pm on 12th February.

The summer practice night is Mondays from 6.30pm at our ground in Northcroft Lane, Newbury.

Junior membership (£35) is open to anyone aged under 17 on 1st September in the preceding year.

To join or to make enquiries please send us an email and one of our club officers will get back with all the information.
