Open day 2015

Saturday, 11th April 2015 at Newbury Cricket Club.

Your chance to get a taste for the summer. Friendly atmosphere, excellent ground, nets, bar...why not come along and join us on a fun filled day.

If you’re a local cricketer looking for a new club, or perhaps you’ve not played in a while and want to put on your whites again, then our Open Day on 26th April 2014 is certainly for you. Likewise, if you’ve ever wanted to know more about cricket, or fancied having a go to see if you’d like to take part more regularly, we’d be delighted to see you.

Throughout the day, you will have the chance to watch demonstration matches from our junior and senior sections, or take part in net sessions. Alternatively, perhaps you see yourself as a big hitter and want to try your luck in our ‘Smashing Sixes’ competition. Whatever you decide, we’d be delighted to welcome you and show you what your local cricket club has to offer.

We are looking for new players, members and supporters. If you need more information, please email us.
